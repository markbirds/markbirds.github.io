Chess and Perdigana

Perdigana, if you didn’t know, is a game similar to checkers or dama (a popular Filipino board game), but it’s the opposite because you win by losing all your pieces. I first learned about it through Sci-Dama, a game with similar rules but has calculations and points, where I even participated in a competition. I actually like Perdigana more than Dama because it’s faster to play and more fun.

Since Perdigana isn’t very popular and it’s hard to find players, even online, I learned chess instead. If you have the time, let’s play!
